**Indonesian Runners Japan (IRJ)** started from a desire to unite runners from Indonesia who are living in Japan. 

Whether you are a casual runner looking to break a sweat on weekends, or a marathoner aiming for a podium finish or a *Personal Best*, this community is a place to support each other, share training routes, and plan participations in various races across Japan.
